Controls

  • Use A and D to move left and right respectively.
  • Use the spacebar to jump.
  • Click on the map to fire against your enemies.

Mission

Attention corporal! Some enemy soldiers have been spotted nearby. We need you to go out there and defeat them. Once the mission is accomplished, you will be able to return back home in the truck. Remember, the truck wont set off until you defeat all of them. Good luck!
